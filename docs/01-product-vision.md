# AI PMO Copilot - Product Vision

## Vision
Create an intelligent PMO platform that combines project management practices, artificial intelligence and automation to increase strategic visibility, operational efficiency and decision quality.

## Objectives
- Reduce manual PMO activities.
- Improve project predictability.
- Enable data-driven executive decisions.
- Create an intelligent layer over project portfolio management.

## Strategic Direction
The AI PMO Copilot evolves from an assistant model into an autonomous PMO capability.
